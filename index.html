The Future of Farming

With the global population projected to reach 9.7 billion by 2050 (UN report), the demand for food is soaring. In India, agriculture's GDP contribution has fallen from 35% in 1990-91 to 15% recently (The Economic Times). Advanced technology is the key to revitalizing this vital sector.

Agro Ratna Elite harnesses cutting-edge innovations to deliver sustainable, efficient, and productive farming solutions.

Why Technology Matters

  • Advanced machinery enhances scale, speed, and productivity.
  • Improved seeds, irrigation, and fertilizers boost crop yields.
  • Smart technologies like robots and sensors promote eco-friendly farming.
  • Digital systems reduce food waste and optimize supply chains.
  • Genetic engineering enhances pest resistance and productivity.
  • Mechanization minimizes manual labor while maximizing efficiency.

Our Solution

Agro Ratna Elite is an AI-powered, multi-task agricultural robot designed to tackle modern farming challenges with innovative, sustainable solutions.

Autonomous Farming

Seamless, fully autonomous farm management for maximum efficiency.

About Agro Ratna Elite

Crafted in the Atal Lab and dedicated to the visionary industrialist, Late Mr. Ratan Tata, Agro Ratna Elite empowers farmers with innovative technology to ensure sustainable and efficient agriculture for a thriving future.

Key Features

1

Automated Ploughing

Precision ploughing powered by a Servo Motor and Arduino Uno, triggered by smart commands.

2

Seed Sowing

Automated seed sowing with a custom-designed bottle system and Servo Motor for accuracy.

3

Smart Irrigation

Real-time soil moisture monitoring with sensors and Arduino Uno for efficient water delivery.

4

Weed Removal

Machine Learning-driven weed detection and removal, controlled by Arduino Uno.

5

Animal Detection

AI system detects animals, sends alerts, and activates a buzzer to protect crops.

6

Thief Detection

Under development: Detects unauthorized persons for enhanced field security.
